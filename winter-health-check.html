Complete Winter Health Check

A comprehensive seasonal check carried out by our experienced technicians in London, designed to keep you safe and offer peace of mind.

What Our Checks Cover

We rigorously inspect 9 critical points of your vehicle to ensure optimum winter performance.

Battery Health Check

Battery failure is one of the most common causes of winter breakdowns. We test the condition of your car battery to reduce the risk of a non-start on cold mornings.

Brake Test (Visual & Roller)

Coming to a stop on time on icy roads is vital. We visually inspect your brakes and use a roller brake tester to test the effectiveness of your braking system.

Fluids & Engine Oil Level

Proper oil levels are essential for engine protection during cold starts. We also check screen wash, anti-freeze, and brake fluid to ensure they are at correct levels.

Light Bulb & Wiper Checks

With shorter days and poor visibility, fully working lights and wipers are essential. We check all exterior lights and wiper blade conditions to maintain clear visibility.

Suspension & Steering Visual

We carry out a visual inspection to identify any obvious issues with your suspension or steering components that could impact safety or stability.

Tyre Health & Pressure

Your tyres are the main contact point between your car and the icy road. We check for hidden defects, tread depth, and make sure your tyre pressure is correct.
